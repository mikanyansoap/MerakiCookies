Project Title:
Meraki Cookies: A Web-Based Ordering System with Database Management

Members:
BENZON, Heidi
FERNANDEZ, Angela
JACINTO, Krizelle
PEDUCA, Sophia

Meraki Cookies is a small business that specializes in producing and selling homemade cookies. Currently, the organization relies on manual methods of receiving orders and managing customer information, which can lead to inefficiencies, order inaccuracies, and difficulties in tracking sales and inventory. To address these challenges, the proposed web-based ordering system with database management aims to streamline the ordering process, improve record keeping, and provide customers with a more convenient and efficient purchasing experience.
