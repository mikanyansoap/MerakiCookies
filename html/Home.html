About Shop

Meraki Cookies is a small cookie business dedicated to creating freshly baked, high-quality cookies made with love. The name "Meraki" is derived from the Greek language which means "to do something with soul, creativity, and love." Our commitment is to put creativity and soul into every batch we make.

Meraki Cookies is owned by Krizelle Jacinto, who started the business with a passion for baking and sharing homemade goods. It was also created to help with her tuition and expenses.

Location

Makati City, Metro Manila

Meraki Cookies currently operates through home-based production and accepts orders online for pickup at Mapua University - Makati.
